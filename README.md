Name: Two Piece

Pirate Persona turn based

Characters
Monkey - (Musician) Support/Healer- 30 Health
Parrot (Captain) - Defense- 40 Health
Cat (Vice-Captain)- Vinny - Attacker- 20 Health

3 levels + Menu
Menu
Pirate ship
Beach
Cave

Attack System:
Each Character has 4 moves/attacks
Monkey:
Accordion- Heal a medium amount of health to one ally
Lute- Deals light damage
Fiddle- Defense Up
Drum- Attack Up
Parrot: 
Distract- Enemies target Parrot for 3 turns
Screech- Enemy Attack Down
Peck- Deals medium Damage
Tickle-Enemy Defense Down
Vinny:
Slash- Deal Heavy Damage
Lick- Heal small amounts of health for allies. Causes stun on enemies 
Scratch- causes medium damage
Rest- Heal all health, but skip next turn
Enemies:
Enemy Pirate-Ship/Cave- Does 3-5 damage to one character.
Crabs-Beach- Does 5-7 damage to one character
Lobster-Beach- Does 2-5 damage to all characters
Bats-Cave- Does 7-10 damage to one character, heal 2-3 health for self

Bosses:
